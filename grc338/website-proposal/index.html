Introduction

Ashlyn Kay Jewlery

Ashlyn Kay is a jewelry store located in Sherwood, Oregon. They sell handcrafted, modern, elegant jewelry designs. They sell to middle class people who are looking to buy a gift for a loved one, or something for themselves. The jewelry is affordable and meant to be really accessible for anyone to want to buy.

Target audience

The people who go on this site are looking for something nice, but not too expensive where it will break the bank.

They want something unique that wouldn't be found at large, chain jewelry stores. They also want their shopping experience to be quick and easy.

About Us

We are a jewlery boutique located in Sherwood, Oregon. Ashlyn Kay Jewelers was founded in 2021 by Ashlyn Mylander. She grew up here, designing jewlery in her spare time, until going to art school in 2017. A recent graduate of Rhode Island School of Design, she is bringing her talents back to her home town to sell beautiufl jewlery.
